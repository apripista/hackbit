Why Delete Account?

Deleting your account permanently removes your data from InsipiraHub. This action is irreversible, so ensure you back up any important posts or information before proceeding. Visit the Delete Account page if you’re certain you want to proceed.

Importance of Updating Your Email

Keeping your email address up to date ensures you receive important notifications, such as password reset links or TFA codes. An updated email also helps maintain account security. Go to the Change Email page to update your email address.

Benefits of Changing Your Profile

Updating your profile, such as your username or profile picture, personalizes your InsipiraHub experience and helps others recognize you. A current profile enhances your engagement with the community. Check the Change Profile page to make updates.

Secure Your Account with a Strong Password and TFA

Changing your password regularly and enabling Two-Factor Authentication (TFA) adds extra layers of security to your account. TFA requires a verification code in addition to your password, protecting against unauthorized access. Visit the Change Password page or enable TFA in your settings.
