Why Account Settings Matter

Managing your account settings allows you to update your personal information, such as your username, email, and password. Keeping this information current ensures you can access your InsipiraHub account securely and receive important notifications. Visit the Account page to review and update your details.

Importance of Privacy Settings

Your privacy settings control who can see your posts, profile, and activity on InsipiraHub. Adjusting these settings helps you protect your personal information and share content only with those you trust. Check the Privacy page to customize your visibility preferences.

Activate Two-Factor Authentication

Two-Factor Authentication (TFA) adds an extra layer of security to your account by requiring a verification code in addition to your password. Enabling TFA protects your account from unauthorized access, especially for sensitive actions like account deletion or password changes. Go to the Account page to enable TFA today!
