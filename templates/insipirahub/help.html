Inspirahub Help Center

Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I verify my account?
    After registering, you’ll receive a verification email with a link. Click the link to verify your email. If you don’t see the email, check your spam folder or request a new link at Request Verification.
  • What is my security PIN?
    Your security PIN is a unique code sent after verification, required to delete your account. Keep it safe. It’s also included in your welcome email.
  • I didn’t receive my verification email. What should I do?
    Check your spam folder. If it’s not there, request a new verification email at Request Verification.
  • How do I use Inspirahub?
    Update your profile, explore posts, connect with others, and share your ideas. Visit Home to start.
  • How do I contact support?
    Email us at intuitivers@gmail.com for assistance.
