Privacy Settings

Activate TFA

Why Enable Two-Factor Authentication?

Two-Factor Authentication (TFA) adds an extra layer of security by requiring a verification code in addition to your password. This protects your account from unauthorized access, especially for sensitive actions like changing your password or deleting your account. Enable it on the Activate TFA page.

Manage Post Visibility

Controlling who can see your posts ensures your content is shared only with your intended audience. Adjust your post visibility settings to make your posts public, private, or visible to specific groups, keeping your privacy in check. Visit your Settings to configure these options.

Control Profile Visibility

Your profile visibility settings determine who can view your profile details, such as your username and profile picture. Setting your profile to private enhances your privacy on InsipiraHub. Check your Settings to customize your profile visibility.
